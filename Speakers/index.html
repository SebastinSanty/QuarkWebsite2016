GUEST LECTURES

Quarks aims to inspire it's attendees by hosting a plethora of Guest Talks by eminent personalities from all over the world.

Over the years, Quark has been privileged by the presence of some of the most distinguished scientists, entrepreneurs and world leaders. Notable personalities include the Former President of India, Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, Nobel Laureates like Sir Harold Kroto and Prof Douglas Osheroff, eminent scientists such as Dr. Chris Phillips and Dr. Vic Hayes, and many more.

Dr. Kumar Mangalam Birla (Chairman, Aditya Birla Group), Dr. C Mohan (IBM Fellow), Nir Merry (VP, Applied Materials Inc.), Prabhu Chawla (Editorial Director, Indian Express), Vikrant Bhargava (Notable Venture Capitalist) and Tamer Nakisci (Designer of Nokia 888) are just few of the many inspiring speakers that have graced Quark.

In 2014, Quark hosted an official Ig Nobel Conclave, featuring talks by five recipients of the famous Ig Nobel Prize. We were also honoured by the presence of Dr. Walter Lewin, Emeritus Professor of MIT, who first delivered a lecture via telepresence, and later visited us for over a week.
