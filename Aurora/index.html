AURORA NITES

Quark 2015 amazed a strong crowd with an ingenious show from the world renowned hypnotist-comedian, Andrew Newton. We were also privileged to have Ian McElhinney (the actor who plays Ser Barristan Selmy on the hit TV series 'Game of Thrones') grace us with his presence and share his experiences with us. With awe-inspiring performances by the likes of Feeding the Fish (performers at the Athens Olympics 2004), world renowned magician Arvind Jayashankar and several more astounding performances in the previous editions, the bar has been set high and Quark 2016 aspires to deliver even better.
