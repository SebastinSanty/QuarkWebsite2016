THE EXHIBITONS

The Exhibitions at Quark have been a medium to showcase one's talent to the audience and serve as a 3-day attraction for the public, giving them a taste of the best technological innovations from across the Globe. In the previous editions of Quark we have had Exhibitions by Nicolas Hutchet, who displayed his “BionicoHand” a bionic hand which he has made for himself, Team AcYut (a humanoid robot project by the students of BITS Pilani), Lorenz Potthast of Germany displayed his “Decelerator Helmet”, The Soundbottle Project by Japanese designer Jun Fujiwara, an Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (AUV) from Delhi Technological University, exhibits from ISRO, DRDO, Team Pratham (a student satellite project) from IIT Bombay, and many more.
